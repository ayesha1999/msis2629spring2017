      Regional Universities West Rankings

      Regional Universities offer a full range of undergraduate programs and some master's programs but few doctoral programs. Regional Universities include Providence College, Santa Clara University, Elon University and Butler University. These rankings are split into four regions: North, South, Midwest and West.
